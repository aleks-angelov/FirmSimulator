Introduction

Welcome to the Microeconomic Simulator of Firm Behavior under Monopolistic Competition!


In this educational game, you will play the role of the CEO of a recently launched startup for a period of three years.


Your goal is to maximize your firm’s profits within the dynamic environment of a monopolistically competitive market.


You will make quarterly decisions about the quantity of goods your firm produces and the amount it invests in product research.


Quantity affects your firm's total revenue and its total cost. Research has a 50% chance of reducing your firm's costs by an amount proportional to that invested.


When you are ready to begin, choose your settings from the drop-down list above and click the Start button to launch the simulation.


WARNING: If you reload/leave the game before you complete it, you will lose your progress!
